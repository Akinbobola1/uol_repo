  • Housing Week Webinar: What To Expect When You Are Moving In

    Date: Monday, May 16, 2022 - 13:00 to 14:00
    Event type: Housing Talks
    College:

    This webinar will cover the basics of what to expect when moving into privately rented accommodation and what your landlord is required to do. There will be a Q&A after the webinar. Students from any subscribing college can attend. C

  • Housing Fair 2022

    Date: Tuesday, May 17, 2022 - 11:00 to 15:00
    Event type: Housing Fair
    College:

    This year we are back to in person! The Housing Fair is your chance to meet different housing providers and find the right accommodation for you.

  • Housing Week Webinar: Disrepair

    Date: Wednesday, May 18, 2022 - 13:00 to 14:00
    Event type: Housing Talks
    College:

    In this webinar we will be looking at common disrepair issues in rented accommodation. We will outline landlord obligations, explain where you can seek help with repair issues that may be hazardous and also provide some tips for how you can prevent some issues from occurring in your property.

  • Housing Week Webinar: Deposits

    Date: Thursday, May 19, 2022 - 13:00 to 14:00
    Event type: Housing Talks
    College:

    A webinar explaining the types of deposit you may be asked to pay when renting private accommodation, how much you should expect to pay, and when your landlord must protect your deposit.

  • Housing Week Webinar: Flatmate Disputes

    Date: Friday, May 20, 2022 - 11:00
    Event type: Housing Talks
    College:

    Disagreements between flatmates will inevitably arise from time to time, but what happens when you can’t reach a resolution? This webinar will look at the strategies and services that can help you to resolve difficult disputes. We’ll consider your rights and responsibilities as a joint tenant and explore the options available for leaving a tenancy early. We'll also have a Q&A to get your questions answered.
