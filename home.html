About Us

University of London Housing Services offers a range of services to assist and support students and University of London staff who are looking for and living in private accommodation in London. Our service for students is not just limited to those studying at University of London member institutions. We also assist students of several external Universities and Colleges. You can see a list of all of our subscribing colleges here.
