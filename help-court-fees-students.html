Joint claims


Groups of students who are making a claim together will have to apply for fee remission assistance individually, and all their circumstances will be considered. If one student from a group does not qualify for any fee assistance, they will need to pay the fee for the case. The fee will be no higher than it would have been had they applied on their own.
